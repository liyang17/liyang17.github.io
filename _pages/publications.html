Publications

Journal Articles

Thermal Performance of Packed-Bed Latent Heat Storage Tank Integrated with Flat-Plate Collectors Under Intermittent Loads of Building Heating

Jiatao Liu, Shilei Lyu*

Energy, Apr. 2024

DOI: 10.1016/j.energy.2024.04.001

Abstract: This study investigates the thermal performance of a packed-bed latent heat storage tank integrated with flat-plate solar collectors for building heating applications. The intermittent load conditions are analyzed to optimize the system's efficiency.

Optimal Design of Air Distribution Systems in Large Indoor Spaces During the Construction of Walls: A Case Study on Liquefied Natural Gas Terminal Storage Tanks

Yan Ding*, Xiao Pan, Xiong Shen, Jianbo Lai, Yan Zhu, Jiatao Liu, Jobaier, Faezeh Pishahang

Journal of Building Engineering, May. 2024

DOI: 10.1016/j.jbe.2024.05.002

Abstract: This paper presents an optimal design methodology for air distribution systems in large indoor spaces, focusing on the construction of liquefied natural gas terminal storage tanks. Computational fluid dynamics (CFD) simulations are used to validate the design.

Quantitative Method and Influencing Factors Analysis of Demand Response Performance of Air Conditioning Load with Rebound Effect

Ran Wang, Xiaoyue Xin, Jiatao Liu, Shilei Lu*, Yunjun Sun, Wenduan Zhao

Building Simulation, Submitted July. 2024

Abstract: This study proposes a quantitative method to analyze the demand response performance of air conditioning loads, considering the rebound effect. The influencing factors are identified through sensitivity analysis.

Conference Papers

Combination of UV Radiation with 3D Structure Media Filter for Indoor Air Disinfection

Jiatao Liu, Junjie Liu*, Yingying Fan, Pan Wang

The 16th ROOMVENT Conference (ROOMVENT 2022), Aug. 2022

Abstract: This paper explores the effectiveness of combining UV radiation with a 3D structured media filter for indoor air disinfection. Experimental results demonstrate significant improvements in air quality.
